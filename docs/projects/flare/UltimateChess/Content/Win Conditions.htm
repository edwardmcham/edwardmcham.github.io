Win Conditions

A player wins when either of these occurs:

  • The opponent's King is captured in a King card battle (best of three).
  • The opponent's King attempts to capture a piece, loses the card battle, and is captured.

Ultimate Chess has no checkmate, stalemate, or draw by repetition. The game ends only when a King is captured.

Note: Standard chess draw conditions — insufficient material, the fifty-move rule, and mutual agreement — apply only as a house rule both players agree to before the game begins.
