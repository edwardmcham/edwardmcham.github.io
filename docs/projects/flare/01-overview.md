# Overview

Ultimate Chess is a variant of standard chess. Captures are never automatic. Every capture attempt triggers a **card battle** — a round of War played with a standard deck. Card luck, timing, and risk management layer on top of positional chess strategy.

This rulebook covers only the rules that differ from standard chess. Standard chess rules govern piece movement, turn order, castling, en passant, and promotion unless noted otherwise.

> **Note:** Each player needs a full standard deck of cards, including both Jokers, before the game begins. See [Dice Variant](07-dice-variant.md) for a card-free option.
