# Sniper Rules

Ultimate Chess calls a Joker a **Sniper**.

## Winning with a Sniper

1. The attacker wins the card battle normally. The target piece is captured.
2. The attacker selects any one non-King piece from the opponent's remaining army and removes it from the board without a card battle.

> **Note:** The bonus piece removal is immediate and requires no additional card play. The attacker chooses freely.

## Armageddon — Both Players Play a Sniper

If both players play a Joker as their face-up card in the same round, Armageddon triggers.

1. Both the attacking piece and the defending piece are immediately removed from the board.
2. Each player selects and removes three non-King pieces from the opponent's army, without card battles.
3. Both players make their selections simultaneously. Don't wait for your opponent to choose first.

> **Important:** Armageddon can dramatically change the board state. If either player has fewer than three non-King pieces remaining, they remove as many as they have.
